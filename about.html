About Our Oraganization

Zip 101 is an organiztion that helps the awarness of bullying within minorities. No acceptance within ethnicity, finacial class, and gender idenification is a huge issue when it comes to bullying. This organization provides bullying videos, local health groups, messaging for friends, online groups, and privater mentors, and more.

We provide the best service that we can to make you feel comfortable to talk through situations, accepted in this world, and have it be known that you can be you no matter what anyone says. The moment you get on this website, we try to make you feel as combtorable as possible. Join us for your daily routine and become apart of the Zip community.
